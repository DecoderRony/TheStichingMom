About The Owner



Mousumi Biswas is a household woman who started "The Stitching Mom" two years back because of her interest in Embroidery. She started it as a hobby designing beautiful Kurta's, Saree's for her family and friends. From there the idea of converting her hobby into a business came into her mind and from there "The Stitching Mom" came into existence. She started it on a small scale but now it's growing day by day and she now even takes orders from in and around India.
